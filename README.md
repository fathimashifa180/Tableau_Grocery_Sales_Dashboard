# Revenue and Inventory Performance Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze revenue, stock performance, and product trends. The dashboard helps identify high-performing products, monitor stock levels, and understand business performance to support data-driven decision-making.

Note: The dataset used for this dashboard was obtained from Kaggle.

## Objectives
- Analyze total revenue, stock, and product performance
- Identify high-revenue and best-selling products
- Track stock received and last order trends
- Understand the relationship between stock quantity and revenue

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Revenue: $344,269.34  
- Total Units Sold: 58,336  
- Total Products: 990  
- Total Stock: 55,053  
- Total Suppliers: 990  
- High Revenue Product: Arabica Coffee  

## Dashboard Features

### Revenue Analysis
- Category-wise revenue comparison
- Identification of high-revenue products
- Revenue distribution across different product categories

### Product Performance Analysis
- Top best-selling products based on units sold
- High-revenue product identification
- Product-level stock, reorder level, and reorder quantity tracking

### Trend Analysis
- Stock received trend over time
- Last order date and order trend monitoring
- Relationship between units sold and stock quantity
